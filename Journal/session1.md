#First Entry for K-Logic Devboard

This was my first PCB project so I spent the first 15-20 minutes researching what they are, what different terms mean and what the different pins on the board do.

After that I started working on the schematic for my devboard based off of the tutorial. Schematic = blueprint for your design before you actually start the design.

USBC for the power Crystal Oscilator for the clock system (didn't know computers know the time from rocks lol) SOC = System on a Chip, brain of the PCB. Flash Storage to store info
